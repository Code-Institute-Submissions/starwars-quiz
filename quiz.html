How to Play

  • Click "Start" to start the game.
  • Once your game starts, you will be shown an image, question and four possible answers.
    - Simply click the answer of your choice.
    - If it's correct, the icons below will let you know if it was right or wrong - remember the images can help with the question, but not always.
  • If at any point you want to exit the quiz, you can click on the exit button at the bottom of the screen.
  • Have fun and may the force be with you!
